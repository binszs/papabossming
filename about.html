About Papaboss Ming Chibugan

More than just silog, we serve memories and home-cooked goodness.

Our Story

Papaboss Ming Chibugan started as a humble roadside eatery with a big dream: to offer hearty, homemade Filipino favorites at prices every Juan can afford. Inspired by traditional recipes and the warmth of Filipino hospitality, we serve every meal with a smile, and a full stomach.

From the classic tapsilog to our rich bulalo and flavorful chixsilog, we are committed to keeping it authentic, delicious, and always lutong bahay. Whether you're a regular or a first-time diner, there's always a plate waiting for you at Papaboss.
